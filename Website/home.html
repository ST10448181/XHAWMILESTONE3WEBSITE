"Empowering the nation" is a training school initiative by Precious Radebe that provides skills training for domestic workers and gardeners. Established in 2018, the program offers six-months and short six-weeks courses designed to upskill individuals, making them more marketable and capable of earning higher wages. Through this training workers gain valuable skills in areas like first aid,sewing,landscaping,child-minding and garden-maintenance,enabling them to improve their employability or even start thier own small businesses
